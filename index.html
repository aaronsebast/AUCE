Tourist Attractions

Auce Old Town: Explore cobblestone streets, traditional Latvian architecture, and quaint cafés offering local delicacies.

Auce Manor: This 19th-century manor is surrounded by lush gardens, ideal for history lovers and photography enthusiasts.

Nature Parks: Several parks around Auce offer trails, wildlife observation, and scenic spots for family outings.

How to Reach Auce

Auce is well-connected by road and rail, making it easily accessible for visitors from Riga and other major cities. Regular train services and buses run to and from Auce.

Travel Time

Auce is approximately 1.5 hours away from Riga by car or train, making it an ideal destination for a weekend getaway.

Weather in Auce

Auce experiences a temperate climate with cold winters and mild summers. The best time to visit is during the spring and autumn months for pleasant weather.

Culture

Auce is rich in Latvian culture, with a variety of local art, music, and traditional events held throughout the year.

History of Auce

Auce has a long and rich history dating back to the Middle Ages. The town is home to many historical buildings, including the Auce Castle and several old churches.

Food & Music

Latvian cuisine is simple yet delicious, featuring hearty dishes like grey peas with bacon, rye bread, and smoked meats. The local music scene blends traditional folk melodies with contemporary sounds.

Geography

Auce is located in the western part of Latvia, surrounded by forests, rivers, and lakes. The town is set amidst picturesque landscapes perfect for outdoor activities.

Events & Festivals

Auce hosts numerous events throughout the year, including traditional Latvian festivals, outdoor concerts, and local art exhibitions.

Economy

Auce's economy is primarily based on agriculture, with local farms producing dairy, grains, and vegetables. The town also thrives on small-scale industries and tourism.

Education

Auce is home to several schools that provide quality education from elementary through high school, with a focus on traditional values and local culture.

Accommodation

There are several guesthouses and small hotels in and around Auce that offer comfortable stays for tourists looking to experience the charm of the town.

Nature & Outdoor Activities

Auce is perfect for nature lovers, offering hiking trails, kayaking, and bird watching. The nearby lakes and forests provide opportunities for relaxation and adventure.
